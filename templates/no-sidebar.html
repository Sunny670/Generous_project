About Generous Givers: A Lifeline for Turkana

Welcome to Generous Givers, where compassion meets action to make a profound impact on the lives of those in need. Our mission is simple yet profound: to provide hope, relief, and support to the resilient people of Turkana, a remote and underserved region in Kenya.

Why Turkana?

Turkana is a place of breathtaking landscapes and resilient communities, but it's also a place where challenges are a daily reality. The Turkana people face extreme poverty, limited access to clean water, healthcare, education, and food insecurity. It's a region where the simple act of survival can be a heroic feat.

Our Commitment

At Generous Givers, we are committed to making a meaningful difference in Turkana.
Our dedicated team and network of generous donors share a common goal: to bring about positive change in this beautiful yet underserved part of northern Kenya.

What We Do

We focus on practical, sustainable solutions that empower Turkana communities:

  • We focus on practical, sustainable solutions that empower Turkana communities:
  • Education Support: Education is a pathway to opportunity. We support schools and students in Turkana to ensure access to quality education.
  • Healthcare Access: We strive to improve healthcare services, making sure the people of Turkana have access to essential medical care and health resources.
  • Nutrition and Food Security: We address food insecurity through innovative projects aimed at improving agriculture and food production.
  • Empowerment Programs: Our initiatives empower individuals and communities, fostering self-reliance and resilience.
